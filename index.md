# Welcome​

<img src="https://github.com/MichaelNebor/Michael-Nebor-Profile/blob/master/assets/images/Nebor_Michael_ProfilePicture.jpg?raw=true" width="50%" hspace="20" align="right">

My name is Michael Nebor, I am a researcher with a Ph.D. in Financial Economics and Econometrics from Northern Illinois University. I am a quantitative researcher working at the intersection of machine learning, probabilistic modeling, and financial econometrics. My research focuses on modeling uncertainty in asset returns and applying reinforcement learning to portfolio allocation problems.

### Background

I am originally from the Chicagoland area. I completed my undergradute studies at the University of Iowa and my graduate studies at Northern Illinois University. 
My fields of specialization are Financial Economics and Econometrics. I am interested in financial research in the areas of credit risk, financial modeling, and investment strategies using AI. My industry experience consists of working at a bank as a quantitative analyst with a focus on financial modeling.
